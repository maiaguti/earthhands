About Me

Hello and Welcome!
My name is Anastasia and I am a potter located in Orange County, California. It's true, I still love playing with mud... some things never change. But now, I turn the mud into the most beautiful vessles. Whether you would like to purchase a unique handmade pottery item, or you would like to play with the clay yourself, you've come to the right place!
